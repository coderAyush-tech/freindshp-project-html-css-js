How We Met

It all began in the summer of 2024 at Coding Age Academy. Five strangers from different backgrounds found themselves assigned to the same programming class. Azad, the graphic designer; Priyangana, the algorithm enthusiast; Vivek, who kept the mood light with jokes; Ankit, the former athlete trying something new; and Ayush, the quiet problem-solver.

What started as awkward conversations about syntax errors quickly blossomed into coffee breaks discussing code, then collaborative projects, and eventually weekend hackathons. Despite their diverse backgrounds, they discovered a connection that went beyond their shared interest in programming.

Their bond was truly tested when Priyangana received a job offer across the country. Instead of letting distance break them apart, they made a pact to stay connected through weekly video calls, collaborative coding repositories, and annual reunions. They also shared a special secret project they were developing together, which kept their friendship strong despite the miles between them.
